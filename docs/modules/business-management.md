# Business Management Module

## Overview

The Business Management module stores all company information for each tenant.

Every tenant can manage their own business profile independently.

---

## Purpose

- Store business information
- Configure company profile
- Manage branding
- Set default currency
- Configure timezone
- Store contact information

---

## Features

- Business Name
- Owner Name
- Company Logo
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Country
- Currency
- Timezone
- Business Address
- Business Status (Active / Inactive)

---
