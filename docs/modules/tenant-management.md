# Tenant Management Module

## Purpose

Each business will have its own isolated workspace.

---

## Features

- Company Registration
- Business Category Selection
- Workspace Creation
- Subscription Plan
- License Key
- Company Settings

---

## Business Categories

- Ecommerce
- Retail Shop
- Wholesale
- Distribution
- Manufacturing
- Pharmacy
- Restaurant
- Service Business
- Education
- Healthcare

---

## Company Information

- Company Name
- Trade License (Optional)
- BIN (Optional)
- Business Type
- Country
- Currency
- Time Zone
